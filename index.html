MCFlow-SE: One-Step Multichannel Speech Enhancement via MeanFlow

Abstract

Most flow-based speech enhancement methods operate on single-channel inputs, failing to exploit spatial information from microphone arrays. We propose MCFlow-SE, a multichannel framework that integrates mean flow theory with multichannel observations for efficient one-step speech enhancement. By predicting average displacements, our method straightens the generative probability path, enabling high-quality inference in a single forward pass (1-NFE).
